About

Built on craft, driven by curiosity.

I'm Drew Golden — a student at Lehigh University studying Business, and a web designer based out of Hingham, Massachusetts. I started building websites because I saw a real gap: too many great local businesses had outdated or nonexistent web presences.

My approach is simple — no page builders, no themes, no shortcuts. Every site I deliver is hand-coded from scratch, designed around who the client actually is and what their customers actually need. That means cleaner code, faster load times, and something that genuinely stands out.

I bring a business-minded perspective to every project. A website isn't just a design exercise — it's a tool that should work hard for its owner. I think about audience, messaging, and goals as much as I think about layout and typography.
